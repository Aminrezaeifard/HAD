# HAD – Human-Decides AI-Designs

> A research project for AI-driven ERP analysis, design, and implementation.

## Overview

HAD (Human-Decides AI-Designs) is a research methodology that combines Business Process Management (BPMN) and Artificial Intelligence to support the design and implementation of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems.

The core philosophy is simple:

> **Humans decide. AI designs.**

Business experts remain responsible for decisions, policies, and approvals, while AI assists in generating system artifacts such as process models, entities, APIs, forms, reports, dashboards, and documentation.

---

## Vision

Build a next-generation methodology where AI accelerates ERP engineering without replacing human decision-making.

---

## Core Principles

* Human-centered decision making
* BPMN as a visual prompt language
* AI-assisted ERP engineering
* Living documentation
* Shared Intelligence. Private Knowledge.
* Design Once. Deploy Anywhere.
