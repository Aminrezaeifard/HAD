# HAD

> **Human Decides. AI Designs.**

HAD is an AI-driven enterprise engineering methodology that redefines how enterprise systems are analyzed, designed, deployed, and continuously improved.

Unlike traditional development approaches, HAD positions Artificial Intelligence as an intelligent engineering assistant while keeping humans in full control of business decisions.

Using BPMN as the foundation, HAD aims to transform business process models into intelligent design prompts that can guide AI in generating enterprise solutions, including workflows, forms, entities, APIs, documentation, and deployment plans.

## Vision

To establish a next-generation methodology for AI-assisted enterprise engineering based on:

* Human-centered decision making
* BPMN-driven design
* Multi-agent collaboration
* Living documentation
* Shared cloud intelligence with private local knowledge

## Core Principles

* Human Decides. AI Designs.
* Shared Intelligence. Private Knowledge.
* Continuous Collective Learning.
* Design Once. Deploy Anywhere.
* Living Documentation.
