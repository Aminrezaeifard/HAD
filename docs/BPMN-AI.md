# BPMN-AI

## Overview

BPMN-AI is an extension of the Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN) that transforms business process models into an intelligent design language for AI-assisted enterprise engineering.

Instead of using BPMN only for process documentation and workflow execution, BPMN-AI enables AI to understand business intent and generate enterprise artifacts under human supervision.

Following the HAD philosophy, AI does not replace human decision-making. Instead, it acts as an intelligent engineering assistant that proposes solutions while humans make the final decisions.

## Objectives

The objectives of BPMN-AI are:

* Transform BPMN into a visual prompt language for AI.
* Generate enterprise forms and user interfaces.
* Design database entities.
* Generate APIs and Web Services.
* Produce reports and dashboards.
* Generate living documentation.
* Support intelligent deployment.
* Enable multi-agent collaboration.

## Design Principle

Human Decides. AI Designs.
