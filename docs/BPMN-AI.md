# BPMN-AI

## Overview

BPMN-AI is an extension of the Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN) that transforms business process models into an intelligent visual prompt language for AI-assisted enterprise engineering.

Unlike traditional BPMN, which focuses on process modeling and execution, BPMN-AI enables Artificial Intelligence to understand business intent, analyze enterprise requirements, and generate implementation artifacts under human supervision.

The philosophy of BPMN-AI follows the HAD methodology:

**Human Decides. AI Designs.**

---

# Vision

The objective of BPMN-AI is to bridge the gap between business analysis and enterprise implementation.

Instead of manually translating business requirements into software specifications, AI interprets BPMN-AI models and proposes enterprise solutions while humans remain responsible for approvals and business decisions.

---

# Why BPMN-AI?

Traditional BPMN is excellent for documenting business processes, but it does not provide enough semantic information for AI-assisted enterprise engineering.

Organizations still spend significant effort translating process models into:

- Forms
- Database entities
- APIs
- Business rules
- Reports
- Dashboards
- Documentation
- Deployment plans

BPMN-AI extends BPMN to automate this engineering process.

---

# Core Objectives

BPMN-AI enables AI to:

- Understand business intent.
- Generate enterprise forms.
- Design database entities.
- Generate APIs and Web Services.
- Produce reports and dashboards.
- Generate Living Documentation.
- Recommend deployment architecture.
- Assist ERP implementation.

---

# BPMN-AI Extensions

The following components extend traditional BPMN.

## AI Service

Allows AI to generate or invoke Web Services and APIs based on business requirements.

---

## Agent Task

Represents activities executed by AI agents instead of human users.

---

## Smart Assignment

An intelligent assignment mechanism.

Supports:

- Static Assignment
- Role-based Assignment
- Relationship-based Assignment
- Selective Assignment
- Smart Assignment

AI dynamically recommends the most appropriate assignee.

---

## AI Advisor

Runs in parallel with human activities.

Analyzes enterprise information and provides recommendations before users make decisions.

---

## AI Explain

Explains:

- Why information is required.
- Where information will be used.
- Which downstream processes depend on it.
- Why fields are mandatory.

This improves transparency and user understanding.

---

## AI Generated Artifacts

Based on BPMN-AI, AI may propose:

- Forms
- Database entities
- APIs
- Web Services
- Reports
- Dashboards
- Documents
- Deployment plans

All generated artifacts require human approval.

---

# Human Approval

Every generated artifact follows the HAD philosophy.

AI proposes.

Humans review.

Humans approve.

Only approved artifacts become part of the enterprise system.

---

# Future Evolution

BPMN-AI is designed as an evolving specification.

Future versions will introduce additional intelligent BPMN components, AI agents, semantic modeling capabilities, and enterprise engineering automation while preserving human governance over business decisions.
