# HAD Architecture

## Overview

The HAD (Human Decides. AI Designs.) architecture is designed as a cloud-assisted, multi-agent enterprise engineering platform.

The architecture separates global intelligence from organization-specific knowledge while ensuring that all critical business decisions remain under human control.

## Core Components

### Cloud Core

The Cloud Core provides shared intelligence, AI guidance, continuous learning, deployment assistance, and platform services.

### Local Runtime

The Local Runtime hosts organization-specific data, business processes, enterprise applications, and private knowledge.

### Multi-Agent Platform

HAD uses specialized AI agents to assist users during the complete enterprise engineering lifecycle, including analysis, design, deployment, documentation, support, and optimization.

### Human Approval Layer

Every important decision requires human review and approval before execution.

### Living Documentation

Documentation is automatically generated and continuously updated throughout the lifecycle of the enterprise system.

## Design Philosophy

* Human Decides.
* AI Designs.
* Agents Execute.
